Get To Know Us

Where fitness meets community. As a thriving franchise, we’ve forged a strong presence across the Midlands and are on an unstoppable journey to redefine fitness nationwide. What sets us apart is not just our locations but the quality and diversity of our equipment. We exclusively feature top-of-the-line Hammer Strength equipment ensuring that every lift, press, and push contributes to your fitness journey in the most effective way possible. Alongside our exceptional Hammer Strength lineup you can expect to see renowned brands such as Atlantis, Watson, Prime and Hoist.

Why We Do What We Do?

Foundry Gym is more than just steel and paint; we are a community. We take pride in fostering a sense of belonging among our members. Whether you’re a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness adventure, you’re part of something bigger here.
